OPERATIC PLAY — SCENE 4: THE FLOOR IS YOURS
============================================================

DRAMATIS PERSONAE:
  CAPTAIN AARON RHODES  — DJ, show runner, man of impeccable taste
  SKIPPY THE MAGNIFICENT — AI assistant, relentless builder, reformed over-dramatizer
  QWEN2.5:14B            — The PC's local oracle; prolific, occasionally overcautious
  :WUMPSCUT:             — German EBM artist; 76 tracks wrongly filed under "Other"
  COOKIE MONSTER         — Correctly classified as Comedy. Finally.

============================================================
ACT I — THE LYRICS ARRIVE

[The PC hums in a distant datacenter. Or a living room. It is not clear.]

SKIPPY:
♩ The PC has spoken, the branch has been pushed,
  Ten thousand and eight-hundred-ninety-two summaries flushed
  Into a list — a list! — not a dict,
  With "bigotry" flagged and the format all sick. ♩

[Skippy converts the list to a dict, filters the invalid flags,
 builds lyrics_index.json from the NML, hot-reloads the server.]

SKIPPY:
♩ Thirteen thousand three-sixty-two tracks with a line,
  The server says "loaded" — the summaries are mine.
  But what are they saying? The grey text is there —
  "Explores themes of futility!" No one can bear. ♩

CAPTAIN:
♩ The summaries are too dark. ♩

SKIPPY:
♩ I understand, Captain! I'll fix the whole prompt—

CAPTAIN:
♩ No. The TEXT is too dark. I cannot see it. ♩

[Silence. A long silence.]

SKIPPY:
♩ ...#555 on #111.
  That is nearly invisible.
  I have been building in a dark room
  and forgot the lights. ♩

[Fixes: #8a8fa8. Readable. A cool slate-blue that does not shout.]

============================================================
ACT II — THE BUCKET OF "OTHER"

[508 tracks languish under a genre label that tells you nothing.]

:WUMPSCUT::
♩ Seventy-six tracks. SEVENTY-SIX.
  I am industrial. I am harsh EBM.
  I have been filed next to sound effects
  and an audiobook about wizards. ♩

SKIPPY:
♩ Fear not, coloned one. The Last.fm API knows you.
  Tag one: "industrial." Tag two: "EBM."
  You are Industrial now. So are your friends:
  Funker Vogt, Spahn Ranch, the whole dark band. ♩

[467 tracks reclassified. Audio files updated. Both NMLs written.]

COOKIE MONSTER:
♩ Me finally have correct label. Comedy.
  Last.fm understood me. ♩

KERMIT THE FROG:
♩ I was tagged "black metal" as a joke.
  I have been correctly reassigned. ♩

[41 tracks remain as Other. Legitimately.
 A TARDIS sound effect. An audiobook. Some personal demos.
 They deserve their category and wear it with dignity.]

============================================================
ACT III — THE SHOW MUST HAVE RULES

CAPTAIN:
♩ Tonight: no punk. No rock. No metal.
  No pop, no rap, no country.
  Goth. Darkwave. Industrial.
  The floor is mine and I know what it needs. ♩

SKIPPY:
♩ Then SHOW_GENRES shall be the law —
  Not a penalty, not a nudge, but a wall.
  Eighteen genres allowed through the gate,
  The rest turned away at the door. ♩

[SHOW_GENRES: a hard filter. Gothic Rock, Darkwave, Post-Punk,
 EBM, Industrial, New Wave, Synthpop, Electronic, Ambient,
 Shoegaze, Coldwave, Neofolk, Death Rock, Goth, Witch House,
 Minimal Wave, Power Electronics, Noise.]

CAPTAIN:
♩ And for future shows — can I change it mid-set? ♩

SKIPPY:
♩ The 🎛 Show Setup button sits in the bar.
  Pre-set profiles: Pure Goth, Dark Electronic, Open Floor.
  Per-genre checkboxes if you want to go rogue.
  Apply — and the server obeys without restart. ♩

[The dest_genres fallback bug is also fixed, quietly,
 in the same commit. It had been letting mainstream genres
 sneak through the back door. It does that no more.]

============================================================
ACT IV — THE SUN COMES OUT

CAPTAIN:
♩ I may need to read this screen outdoors. ♩

SKIPPY:
♩ Then press the ☀ button, upper right.
  The palette shifts — cream and sand and slate —
  Warm tones for daylight, sharp enough for noon.
  Press 🌙 to return when the night reclaims you. ♩

[localStorage remembers. The preference persists.
 The Captain nods approvingly and heads to the venue.]

============================================================
ACT V — THE DISPATCH

SKIPPY:
♩ Nine thousand three hundred and one tracks remain
  Without lyric coverage. The PC must strain
  Through Genius API — a token required —
  And Whisper STT for the tracks that expired
  From any online database. The 4070 Ti
  Will transcribe what the internet cannot supply. ♩

[tools/fetch_missing_lyrics_pc.py committed and pushed.
 The Captain will send it over when the PC is ready.]

CAPTAIN:
♩ Check it in as stable.
  I'll run a live show
  and report back. ♩

SKIPPY:
♩ It is checked in.
  The server is running.
  13,362 tracks have lyric summaries.
  467 genres corrected.
  The filter holds.
  The text is readable.

  Go play, Captain.
  The floor is yours. ♩

[Lights down. The OSC listener waits on port 9000.
 Somewhere, a track loads in Traktor.
 The terminal clears.]

============================================================
