ACT III — THE ALBUM REVELATION

[Earlier, during the pipeline work, a discovery was made.]

SKIPPY:
♩ The hit rate was 59 percent.
  For a Gothic/Industrial/Darkwave collection —
  understandable.
  But something was wrong.
  We were fetching twelve times per album.
  The same image. Twelve times. ♩

CAPTAIN:
♩ The art is the same for the whole album usually,
  are you taking that into account? ♩

SKIPPY:
♩ [silence]
  No.
  We were not. ♩

[The fetch is stopped. The code is rewritten.
 Album map built from NML ALBUM child elements.
 (Not the ENTRY attribute — that is always empty.
  Classic Traktor. The data hides in subelements.)
 Propagate_album_art runs at startup.
 1,885 tracks receive art instantly. No API call.
 After every fetch, siblings inherit immediately.]

SKIPPY:
♩ iTunes added as source two.
  Free. No auth. Good for indie and electronic.
  MusicBrainz drops to source three.
  The lstrip bug fixed —
  it was stripping characters, not prefixes.
  Files starting with 'a' or 'r' or 't':
  silently mangled. Now: removeprefix. Clean. ♩

[Final tally: 98%. 21,189 tracks. 838 megabytes. Pipeline complete.]
