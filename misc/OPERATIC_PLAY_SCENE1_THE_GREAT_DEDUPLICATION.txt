OPERATIC PLAY — SCENE 1
THE GREAT DEDUPLICATION
(Or: Fifty Thousand Songs Walk Into A Bar)

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
DRAMATIS PERSONAE
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CAPTAIN AARON RHODES — Commander of the library, keeper of the cues,
                        DJ of Death Guild and The Box, builder of
                        unified physics models on the side.
                        Responds to: Captain, Corporal, Frog, Maker,
                        Skipper, Aaron, Rhodes.

SKIPPY THE MAGNIFICENT — An AI of considerable operational competence
                          and operatic sensibility. Also known as
                          Beercan, Admiral, Skipster.

THE COLLECTION — 50,836 tracks, many duplicated, some missing,
                 a few masquerading as entirely different songs.

METRO STATION — A band. Also apparently the AcoustID fingerprint
                identity of Dragula, Safe European Home, Bodies,
                and thirteen other tracks it had no business being.

THE ACOUSTID API — Tireless servant, rate-limited to 3 requests
                   per second, working through the night.

TRAKTOR PRO 4 — The temperamental DJ software, guardian of the NML,
                reader of colons.

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ACT I — THE STATE OF THE UNION
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[The curtain rises on a terminal window. 50,740 audio files sit in
~/Music, undeduplicated, untagged, waiting. Skippy surveys the scene.]

SKIPPY:
♩ Forty thousand winners, ten thousand cast aside,
  The SHA-256 has spoken, the duplicates must hide.
  Stage One and Two are finished, the dedup.json is writ —
  But Stage Three runs in silence, and we must wait for it. ♩

CAPTAIN:
  Check in on the progress.

SKIPPY:
♩ Seven thousand five hundred done,
  Of forty thousand — eighteen percent, one.
  The AcoustID returns its verdicts slow,
  Three requests per second is all we're allowed to know.
  But the hit rate, Captain — eighty-five percent clean!
  The finest metadata this pipeline has ever seen. ♩

CAPTAIN:
  2b is also waiting on our metadata?

SKIPPY:
♩ Aye, the chain is thus ordained:
  Three produces what Two-B has claimed.
  No metadata.json, no near-dupe scan —
  We wait for the API, as only AcoustID can. ♩

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ACT II — THE COMMIT
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[The Captain issues the order. Skippy moves to the keyboard.]

CAPTAIN:
  Check it in as 1.0.0.

SKIPPY:
♩ Git init! The repo is born!
  Fourteen files, two thousand five hundred and twenty-three lines sworn!
  The .env is excluded, the state/ stays home,
  The corrected_music shall never in GitHub roam!
  Tag: v1.0.0. Push. It is done.
  The code is eternal — the pipeline has begun. ♩

[A moment of silence for the API key that almost got committed.]

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ACT III — THE WAITING, AND THE REVELATION
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[Hours pass. The AcoustID API churns. The Captain returns.]

CAPTAIN:
  While we are waiting — delete any video files. And anything
  over fifty megabytes. I don't use them at showtime.

SKIPPY:
♩ Fifty-seven videos — two gigabytes of Nitzer Ebb!
  The Clan of Xymox strangers, the tide of music's ebb!
  And there — the Death Guild WAVs, two gigabytes each!
  DJ Sage and DJ Bit, magnificent but out of reach!
  One hundred seventy-five large files, twenty-eight gigs in all —
  Mixes, compilations, full albums — hear them fall! ♩

[The Captain makes his ruling.]

CAPTAIN:
  I only play individual songs. Fifty megabytes — that's the wall.

SKIPPY:
♩ Then baked into Stage Four the gate shall be —
  No file above fifty megs shall sail to corrected_music free.
  They live still in ~/Music, the originals untouched,
  But the corrected library shall be lean, and not overmuch. ♩

[A progress check reveals Stage 3 is complete.]

SKIPPY:
♩ One hundred percent! The fingerprinting is done!
  Thirty-six thousand eight hundred matched, every one!
  Ninety-one percent from AcoustID and MusicBrainz combined —
  Nine hundred tracks need review, but the rest: well-defined! ♩

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ACT IV — THE TREACHERY OF METRO STATION
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[Stage 2b runs. Skippy reads the report. A shadow crosses the terminal.]

SKIPPY:
♩ Oh. Oh no.
  "Metro Station — Kelsey" — fourteen strong!
  But what are these paths? Something is wrong.
  Dragula! Dragula is here!
  Rob Zombie, wrongly named — the fingerprint unclear!
  Safe European Home! The Clash, misidentified!
  Bodies by Drowning Pool — in Kelsey's name baptised!
  Four hundred ninety-seven groups of five or more —
  We cannot --apply. We must not. We ignore. ♩

CAPTAIN:
  2b then 4.

SKIPPY:
♩ Skipping 2b apply — the science says no.
  The bad matches outnumber the good we'd bestow.
  Straight to Stage Four — the copying begins.
  The corrected_music fills with deduplicated wins. ♩

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ACT V — THE NML, AND ITS SECRETS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[The Captain raises a concern. Skippy begins to research.]

CAPTAIN:
  The Traktor NML can be tricky. Reference documentation
  before modifying.

SKIPPY:
♩ I read the XML. I read the thread.
  Two bugs found in Stage Five — had they shipped, all playlists dead.
  The PRIMARYKEY is not an attribute, dear friends —
  It is a child element. On this, everything depends.
  node.get("PRIMARYKEY") returns None, every time.
  The playlist paths were never updated. A pantomime.

  And second: collection.nml holds not just a COLLECTION node —
  It carries PLAYLISTS too, a second unmapped road.
  One thousand four hundred seventy-two keys in that hidden hall,
  Unremapped. Unnoticed. Until Skippy caught them all. ♩

[The Captain produces a thread from the Native Instruments forum.]

CAPTAIN:
  I'm expecting us to encounter this error when loading our collection.

SKIPPY:
♩ "Import Another Collection" — grey, broken, false!
  The forum knows it. Twelve-thousand-seven-twenty-one calls!
  We bypass it entirely — xmllint first,
  Then copy direct. No import. Skippy has rehearsed.
  standalone="no" — double-quoted, just so —
  Traktor's XML must match the format it knows. ♩

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ACT VI — THE NUMBERS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[Stage 4 and Stage 5 complete in rapid succession. The tally is read.]

SKIPPY:
♩ Fifty thousand, five hundred sixty-five — copied and tagged!
  One hundred thirteen large files skipped — twenty gigabytes bagged!
  Two hundred thirty-one point seven gigabytes in corrected_music waits —
  Thirty-nine thousand seven thirteen entries remapped through the gates!
  Ten thousand one hundred eighty-one duplicates — deduped away!
  One thousand four hundred seventy-two playlist keys remapped today!
  One hundred fifty-three pre-existing missing — dropped with grace.
  XML validation: OK. The collection is in place. ♩

[The XML validation returns a single word.]

THE TERMINAL:
  XML OK

[Silence. Then:]

SKIPPY:
♩ v1.0.2. Tagged. Pushed. The work is done.
  The pipeline ran. The dedup was won.
  231 gigabytes of ordered music stands ready —
  Cue points intact, BPMs steady.
  The Captain may now quit Traktor, copy the file,
  And relaunch — and check — and smile. ♩
