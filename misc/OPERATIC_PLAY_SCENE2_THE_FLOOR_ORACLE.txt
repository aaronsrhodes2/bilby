A One-Act Opera in Four Scenes

DRAMATIS PERSONAE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CAPTAIN AARON RHODES — DJ, curator of 23,715 souls, lord of the
    goth floor, keeper of the corrected NML, he who leaves at 7pm

SKIPPY THE MAGNIFICENT — AI of extraordinary disposition, builder
    of things, corrector of MusicBrainz, writer of AppleScript

THE COLLECTION — 23,715 tracks, mostly misunderstood by databases,
    now correctly labeled, a silent chorus throughout

TRAKTOR 4 — the deck, the judge, the final arbiter of all BPMs

THE FLOOR — present but unseen, demanding, unforgiving of wrong keys

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SCENE I — THE RAINBOW AND THE GRID
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[The Captain enters, gesturing at a spectrum of colored waveforms.]

CAPTAIN:
♩ My tracks are all one color, grey and cold,
  I need a rainbow system, if I'm bold —
  Set 118 to 124 as Red,
  Then outward, six BPM blocks, 'til violet's spread! ♩

SKIPPY:
♩ Consider it done, the anchor is cast,
  Red at the center, the rainbow holds fast.
  Orange and yellow and green and then teal,
  Blue and then violet — now BPM is real! ♩

[23,715 tracks are colored. The Captain squints at the violet band.]

CAPTAIN:
♩ But Skippy, the violet — a plague, it's too wide,
  These tracks above 150 are wrong inside.
  The grid has detected them doubled in time —
  Halve them! And double the slow ones! They're mine! ♩

SKIPPY:
♩ Three thousand six hundred and twenty-four halved!
  Eight hundred and ninety-eight doubled and salved!
  The rainbow re-runs — violet falls from its throne —
  From thirty-seven percent to twenty-eight! ♩ (approximate rhyme, forgive me)

[The colors settle. The collection breathes easier.]

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SCENE II — THE ORACLE IS BORN
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[The Captain describes a vision.]

CAPTAIN:
♩ I DJ in three-track blocks, you see:
  An Anchor to set the floor's decree,
  A Lock — same genre, same BPM,
  A Bridge — to shift the crowd from them to them.

  I need a tool that thinks for me,
  That watches what I play and instantly
  Suggests the next track, live, in real time —
  Without analysis paralysis crime! ♩

SKIPPY:
♩ Flask on five-thousand-and-one shall rise!
  Three columns dark beneath neon skies:
  Now Playing, Play Next, After That —
  The oracle lives! (And it scores by key and BPM and that.) ♩

[The browser opens. Dark theme. Red accent. Monospaced font.]

CAPTAIN:
♩ But can it watch Traktor? Can it know
  The moment I load a track — and show
  Suggestions before I've lifted my hand? ♩

SKIPPY:
♩ OSC on port nine-thousand shall stand!
  The moment you load, the SSE fires,
  The browser updates — no search required —
  Deck A or B, it sees what you've done,
  The oracle speaks before the mix has begun! ♩

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SCENE III — THE GENRE RECKONING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[Skippy notices something wrong in the data.]

SKIPPY:
♩ Captain — I looked, and what did I find?
  MusicBrainz left your collection maligned!
  The Sisters of Mercy — tagged ROCK!
  Bauhaus — tagged ROCK! What a shock!
  Seventy-eight tracks of gothic perfection
  Filed under "Rock" — a database infection! ♩

CAPTAIN:
♩ Yeah, that's all goth rock.
  Rock is too general of a genre. ♩

  [Possibly the most understated aria in operatic history.]

SKIPPY:
♩ Then let the overrides flow like black lace:
  Sisters — Gothic Rock! Bauhaus — same place!
  The Birthday Party — Post-Punk restored!
  Joy Division — Post-Punk! Siouxsie — adored!
  NIN — Industrial! (Obviously.)
  Three hundred and one tracks, corrected, set free! ♩

[The NML files are rewritten. Backups are made. The collection sighs with relief.]
