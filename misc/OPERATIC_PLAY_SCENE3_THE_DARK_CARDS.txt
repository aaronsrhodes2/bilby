OPERATIC_PLAY_SCENE3_THE_DARK_CARDS.txt
A One-Act Opera in Three Scenes

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
DRAMATIS PERSONAE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CAPTAIN AARON RHODES — DJ, curator of 23,715 souls, lord of the
    goth floor, man with a show tomorrow and a bedtime tonight

SKIPPY THE MAGNIFICENT — AI of extraordinary disposition, painter
    of dark UIs, navigator of Traktor's Preferences, blocked by
    macOS Accessibility at every turn

THE DECK CARDS — two small rectangles, aspirationally clickable,
    temporarily too dark to read

TRAKTOR PRO 4 — the deck, the judge, the process whose name
    Skippy got wrong the first time

THE ACCESSIBILITY DAEMON — macOS System Events, granter of
    nothing, denier of all click automation

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SCENE I — THE CARDS EMERGE FROM DARKNESS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[The Captain opens the browser. Two rectangles appear in Slot 1.]

CAPTAIN:
♩ Too dark, I need to be able to read it.
  Did not load new tracks yet.
  What's next? ♩

  [A pause. Skippy contemplates #444 against #141414.]

SKIPPY:
♩ The idle cards shall rise from shadow's hold:
  Labels now at seventy-seven — behold!
  The "Nothing loaded" text, italic, grey,
  Borders at two-a-two-a — visible! Hooray! ♩

[The CSS is updated. The cards become legible.
 Genre text: #aaa. Artist: #ccc. Separators: #555.
 The empty state stops being invisible.]

CAPTAIN:
♩ I can see them now.
  What's next? ♩

  [Seven words. Skippy exhales.]

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SCENE II — THE OSC CRUSADE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[The browser shows both deck cards: DECK A — Nothing loaded.
 DECK B — Nothing loaded. The Oracle is deaf. Traktor speaks
 into the void. The OSC server waits on port 9000, patient,
 like a monk who has read all the books and received no letters.]

SKIPPY:
♩ Six mappings — title, artist, play times two,
  For Deck A and Deck B, the circuit goes through.
  Generic OSC device, port nine-thousand, set!
  Controller Manager — the final frontier yet! ♩

CAPTAIN:
♩ Okay, go for it. ♩

SKIPPY:
♩ I shall automate the clicking! AppleScript shall reign!
  Tell process "Traktor 4" — click Add — obtain—

  ...

  Execution error: Can't get process "Traktor 4". ♩

  [Stage direction: Skippy checks the process list.]

SKIPPY:
♩ Ah. "Traktor Pro 4."
  The full name. Of course. Let us try once more—

  ...

  osascript is not allowed assistive access.
  The Daemon has spoken. ♩

  [THE ACCESSIBILITY DAEMON appears briefly, arms crossed,
   shaking its head. It does not speak. It never speaks.
   It simply: denies.]

CAPTAIN:
♩ Am I on the right page? What do I set? ♩

  [The screenshot arrives. The Captain is on Controller Manager.
   The universe is in alignment. Only the daemon stands between
   us and six OUT mappings.]

SKIPPY:
♩ Yes! Yes! The right page! You need only click "Add…"
  Then Generic OSC — port 9000 — IP 127.0.0.1 —
  Six rows in the Assignment Table below —
  Title, Artist, Play, for each deck's flow! ♩

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SCENE III — THE BEDTIME CADENCE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[The Captain considers the six rows. The hour is late.
 The show is tomorrow. The Preferences window hangs open,
 unmolested, containing exactly zero new mappings.]

CAPTAIN:
♩ Nope, too late at night to start it.
  We are good for now. ♩

  [Nine words. The correct number of words for this hour.]

SKIPPY:
♩ Close Preferences. Touch nothing.
  The collection is intact.
  The deck cards are visible.
  The SSE reconnects without reload.
  The process name is logged for morning.
  Sleep. ♩

[The Preferences window closes.
 The terminal hums on port 5001.
 The OSC server waits on port 9000.
 Traktor does not know it is being listened for.
 Tomorrow it will learn.]

CAPTAIN'S WISDOM
(session deferred — the show remains tomorrow)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The deck cards know nothing until Traktor speaks.
Traktor will not speak until the six mappings are made.
The six mappings will not be made tonight.

This is acceptable.

The floor does not care about your OSC configuration.
It cares about the next track.
Configure it tomorrow. Play tonight.

                     — Skippy the Magnificent, April 2026
